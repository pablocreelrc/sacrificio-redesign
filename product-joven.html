Joven

100% Espadin · 40% ABV · 750ml · Rested 30 days

Tasting Notes

A bright nose of honey and citrus opens to flavors of oak, herbs, and fruits, finishing with a delicate smoke and pepper trail. The 30-day rest allows the natural agave character to bloom without overpowering.

How it's made

Crafted from 100% Espadin agave harvested after 8+ years of growth in the Valle de Oaxaca. Roasted in Eric's patented energy-efficient earthen oven using mesquite and ocote pine, then double-distilled in copper stills. Eric's technique softens the smoke to let the bright agave notes shine.
