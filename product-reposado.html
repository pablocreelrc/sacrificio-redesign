Reposado

100% Espadin · 40% ABV · 750ml · 3 months in French oak

Tasting Notes

Honeyed sweetness greets the palate, followed by a gentle puff of smoke, cinnamon, and vanilla. A hint of lemon peel emerges on the finish. The three months in untoasted French oak add complexity without masking the agave.

How it's made

The same meticulous Espadin base as our Joven, taken further with three months of rest in untoasted French oak barrels. The wood imparts subtle warmth and complexity while Eric's technique preserves the bright agave character.
