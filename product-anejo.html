Añejo

Espadin + Barril · 45% ABV · 750ml · 1 year in French oak

Tasting Notes

Full, cooked agave opens to layers of chocolate, brown sugar, and cocoa. The finish is smoky and drying, with a warmth that lingers. A year in French oak transforms this into something extraordinary.

How it's made

Our only blend — Espadin for brightness and Barril for depth. After double distillation, the liquid rests for a full year in French oak barrels, developing the rich chocolate and brown sugar notes that define this expression.
